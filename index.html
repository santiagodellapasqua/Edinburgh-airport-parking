Edinburgh Airport is Scotland's busiest airport and the sixth busiest in the UK, serving over 14 million passengers annually. Located 8 miles west of Edinburgh city centre, it's the primary gateway to Scotland's capital and beyond.

Frequently Asked Questions About Edinburgh Airport Parking

Got questions? We've got answers. Here's everything you need to know about parking at Edinburgh Airport.

How much does parking cost at Edinburgh Airport? +

Parking prices at Edinburgh Airport vary by duration and parking type. Our cheapest option is NCP Scotpark at ¬£22.75/week (8 days), compared to ¬£220 if you turn up on the day at the official long stay. For 4 days, prices start from ¬£11.50, and 6 days from ¬£17.25.

Meet & Greet options start from ¬£69.99/week. Always book in advance for the best prices ‚Äì on-the-day rates can be up to 90% more expensive.

What is the cheapest parking at Edinburgh Airport? +

NCP Scotpark and PremiAir Parking consistently offer the cheapest rates at ¬£22.75/week (8 days) or ¬£2.84/day. Both are Park & Ride services located less than 1 mile from the terminal with quick 5-10 minute shuttle transfers.

For even cheaper parking, book well in advance (2+ weeks) as prices increase closer to your travel date.

Can I cancel my Edinburgh Airport parking booking? +

Yes! All our Edinburgh Airport parking bookings include free cancellation up to 24 hours before your arrival time. Simply manage your booking online or contact our customer service team.

Some premium options offer even more flexible cancellation policies. Check your booking confirmation for specific terms.

Is there free parking at Edinburgh Airport? +

Yes, the official Long Stay car park offers 30 minutes FREE parking, making it ideal for quick drop-offs and pick-ups. After 30 minutes, charges apply (¬£7 for 30 mins - 1 hour).

The Long Stay car park is a 6-8 minute walk from the terminal. There's no shuttle service, so factor this in if you have heavy luggage or mobility issues.

Do I need to book parking at Edinburgh Airport in advance? +

While you can turn up on the day at official car parks, we strongly recommend booking in advance to:

  • Save up to 90% vs on-the-day prices
  • Guarantee your space (car parks can fill up during peak times)
  • Access off-airport Park & Ride options (which are cheaper but require pre-booking)
  • Book Meet & Greet services (not available on the day)

Our cheapest pre-book rate is ¬£22.75/week vs ¬£220/week turning up on the day ‚Äì that's a ¬£197 saving!

How early should I arrive at Edinburgh Airport? +

Edinburgh Airport recommends:

  • UK/Ireland flights: 2 hours before departure
  • European flights: 2-3 hours before departure
  • Long-haul flights: 3-4 hours before departure

Add extra time if parking off-airport (allow 15-20 minutes for Park & Ride shuttle) and during peak periods like school holidays, Friday evenings, and Sunday afternoons.

What is Park & Ride at Edinburgh Airport? +

Park & Ride means you drive to a car park located slightly away from the airport (usually 0.5-2 miles), park your car, and catch a free shuttle bus to the terminal. Transfer times range from 2-15 minutes depending on the car park.

It's the most economical option, with prices starting from ¬£22.75/week. All Park & Ride services include:

  • Secure parking with 24/7 CCTV
  • Free shuttle buses (running continuously)
  • Park Mark security accreditation
  • Number plate recognition technology
What is Meet & Greet parking at Edinburgh Airport? +

Meet & Greet (also called valet parking) is a premium service where you drive directly to the terminal, hand your keys to a professional chauffeur, and walk straight to check-in. The chauffeur parks your car in a secure off-site compound while you're away.

On your return, your car is brought back to the terminal for you. No shuttle buses, no waiting ‚Äì ultimate convenience. Prices start from ¬£69.99/week.

Perfect for: Families with young children, business travellers, anyone with lots of luggage, or those seeking maximum convenience.

What if I arrive earlier or later than my booked time at Edinburgh Airport? +

Arriving early: Most car parks will accommodate you if you arrive a few hours early. However, if you're arriving significantly earlier (e.g., the previous day), contact the car park in advance.

Arriving late: Car parks typically have a grace period. If you're running late, call the car park to inform them.

Returning late: If your return flight is delayed, don't worry. Most car parks offer flexibility, though you may incur additional charges for extended stays. Late Return Cover is available for ¬£5-10 to protect against unexpected delays.

Pro tip: Add Late Return Cover to your booking for peace of mind if your flight is delayed up to 48 hours.

No, parking isn't free for extended stays, but Blue Badge holders get 1 hour free parking at all official Edinburgh Airport car parks for drop-offs and pick-ups.

What is the difference between on-airport and off-airport parking? +

On-airport parking is located within the airport perimeter. Options include Multi-Storey, Terminal Surface, Mid Stay, Long Stay, and Plane Parking. These are walking distance from the terminal (1-8 minutes).

Off-airport parking is located outside the airport boundary (0.5-2 miles away). You park and catch a free shuttle bus to the terminal (2-15 minutes). Off-airport is typically cheaper.

Both options offer excellent security. The choice comes down to budget vs convenience.

Are there height restrictions at Edinburgh Airport car parks? +

Multi-Storey Car Park: 2.2m height limit (covered parking)

All other car parks: No height restrictions (open-air or high barriers)

If you're driving a high vehicle (motorhome, van, 4x4 with roof box), avoid the Multi-Storey and book Terminal Surface, Long Stay, or any off-airport Park & Ride instead.

What happens if my car is damaged while parked at Edinburgh Airport? +

While extremely rare due to excellent security, if you discover damage:

  1. Take photos of the damage immediately
  2. Report it to car park staff before leaving the site
  3. Request an incident report form
  4. Contact the car park operator's claims department
  5. If booked through us, contact our customer service team for support

All our partner car parks have liability insurance, though terms vary. Many Meet & Greet services include complimentary insurance coverage.

Can I get a refund if I don't use my parking booking? +

If you cancel at least 24 hours before your booked arrival time, you'll receive a full refund with our free cancellation policy.

If you simply don't turn up (no-show), you won't automatically receive a refund, but contact our customer service team ‚Äì we may be able to help depending on circumstances.
